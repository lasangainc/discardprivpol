Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy, here you will find all information about the data you enter in your app and what is done with it.

Information we collect.

All information you enter on into DisCard, whether its the name of a Space or the content of a note, absolutely everything is stored on device. This local-only approach ensures that your information remains private and secure, and fully under your control. We do not collect or access any of your content, meaning your notes and spaces stay with you and no one else. This means that if the app is uninstalled and then reinstalled, all data will be lost.
